The challenge & our answer

Retrospectives that actually work

83% of Scrum Masters have had no formal facilitation training — yet they're expected to run high-quality retrospectives every sprint.

The problem

  • Teams struggle with consistently executing valuable retrospectives
  • 83% of Scrum Masters have had no formal facilitation training
  • Inconsistent quality of retrospectives within organizations
  • Time constraints for retrospective session preparation
  • Insufficient follow-up on improvement items from previous retrospectives

Our answer

  • Standardized processes: Same expertise and approach within the entire organization
  • Automated support: Cheaper, faster and better than manual processes
  • Flexible approaches: Variations in workflow and session setup
  • Tracking & Benchmarking: Measurable progress and continuous improvement
  • User-friendliness: No dependency on skills or experience
